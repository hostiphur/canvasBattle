Spawn your tank by clicking on the map. Move with a,s,d,w. Shoot with left mouse button. The tank will fire at your cursor. If you are accessing the site the same time as someone else you should be able to try and destroy each other. For the multiplayer to work please use Firefox 4, the latest version of Google Chrome or Safari. Chrome seems to offer the best experience.
